## Appendix: Glossary

**Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable person.

**Data Controller:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.

**Data Processor:** The entity that processes data on behalf of the data controller.

**Data Subject:** The person whose personal data is being collected or processed.

**Consent:** Freely given, specific, informed indication of agreement to data processing.

**Encryption:** Converting data into coded form to prevent unauthorized access.

**Hashing:** One-way encryption (cannot be reversed) used for passwords.
